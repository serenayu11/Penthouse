Meet The Characters

The Plot

The residents of Hera Palace, a luxury penthouse apartment with 100 floors, have many secrets and hidden ambitions. Sim Su Ryeon, who was born into wealth, is the queen of the penthouse apartment. Cheon Seo Jin, the prima donna of the residence, does all she can to give everything to her daughter. Oh Yoon Hee comes from a poor family background, but she strives to enter high society by becoming the queen of the penthouse, the pinnacle of success in her eyes.

Main Characters

Eugene as Oh Yoon-hee

A woman whose dreams have been stunted by her lack of money and power. With gritted teeth, she had to take on any job and humiliation to support her daughter's ambitions.

Lee Ji-ah as Shim Su-ryeon

A grateful, dignified woman who is humane and kind, despite her immense wealth who has a tragic past. She and her husband live in the penthouse, making her the queen of the social clique at Hera Palace.

Kim So-yeon as Cheon Seo-jin

A wealthy woman who is the spitome of showy and arrogant and who harbors warped ambitions. She is a woman who would do anything for power, money and pride.

Shin Eun-kyung as Kang Ma-ri

A woman who has recently come into wealth and leads a secretive double life. She lies to everyone in Hera Palace that her Husband, Yoo Jenny's father, is in Dubai, but in reality, he is in prison.

Um Ki-joon as Joo Dan-tae

The main antagonist of the series. Former husband of Shim Su-ryeon. Former lover of Na Ae-kyo, current husband of Cheon Seo-jin.

Yoon Jong-hoon as Ha Yoon-cheol

Former husband of Choen Seo-jin and current husband and first love of Oh Yoon-hee. He is the head of surgery in the VIP department of a hospital.

Bong Tae-kyu as Lee Kyu-jin

The only son and momma's boy of a wealthy family of judges and lawyers, and is himself a lawyer.

Kim Hyun-soo as Bae Ro-na

Ha Yoon-cheol and Oh Yoon-hee's daughter. She is a gifted soprano singer, wants to do herr best in school and enter the Seoul National University.
