Penthouse: War In Life

About War In Life

War In Life is a South Korean television series starring Lee Ji Ah, Kim So Yeon, Eugene, Uhm Ki Joon, Park Eun Seok, and Yoon Jong Hoon. The series, directed by Joo Dong Min and written by Kim Soon Ok, spins the story of real estate and education war, a desire to be number one. It depicts the solidarity and revenge of women who had no choice but to become evil women to protect themselves and their children. It premiered on SBS TV on October 26, 2020 and airs every Monday and Tuesday at 22:20 KST. The first look teaser was released on September 29.
